# HCCA — Incorporation & Charity Registration Checklist

> **[OFFICIAL — PRIMARY ENGLISH VERSION]** This is the legally operative version for official submission, regulatory reporting, and governance purposes. For the complete Chinese reference translation, see [`registration_checklist_ZH_REF.md`](./registration_checklist_ZH_REF.md). **In case of any discrepancy between the English and Chinese versions, this English version prevails.**

> **Reference:** Incorporated Societies Act 2022 | Charities Act 2005 | Charities Services guidelines
>
> Complete ALL items before submitting to the Registrar of Incorporated Societies and Charities Services.

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Incorporation / 注册前准备

### 1.1 Society Name
- [ ] Proposed name **"NZ Chinese Heritage & Community Alliance Incorporated"** is available (search the Companies Office register).
- [ ] Name ends with "Incorporated" or "Inc." as required.
- [ ] No existing society or company has the same or confusingly similar name.

### 1.2 Membership Requirement
- [ ] Society has **at least 10 members** (s.7 ISA 2022 — minimum membership threshold).
- [ ] Founding members list prepared with full legal names and contact details.
- [ ] Each founding member has signed the membership consent form.

### 1.3 Constitution (Rules)
- [ ] Constitution satisfies **all 12 requirements of Section 26** ISA 2022:
  - [ ] (a) Name of the society
  - [ ] (b) Purposes of the society (charitable, with cultural heritage focus)
  - [ ] (c) How members may be admitted and cease to be members
  - [ ] (d) Subscriptions payable by members (if any)
  - [ ] (e) Rights and obligations of members
  - [ ] (f) How general meetings are called and conducted
  - [ ] (g) How the committee is appointed and removed
  - [ ] (h) Powers and duties of the committee
  - [ ] (i) How the constitution may be amended
  - [ ] (j) How disputes are resolved (Natural Justice principles)
  - [ ] (k) Society not conducted for financial gain of members
  - [ ] (l) Distribution of assets on winding up (to qualifying not-for-profit)
- [ ] Constitution reviewed by a New Zealand solicitor.
- [ ] Constitution adopted at a constitutive general meeting.
- [ ] Meeting minutes recording adoption of constitution signed by Chairperson and Secretary.

### 1.4 Officers / Committee
- [ ] At minimum: President, Secretary, and Treasurer identified.
- [ ] All officers meet **Section 47** eligibility requirements (see [Committee Eligibility Policy](../governance_&_officers/committee_eligibility.md)).
- [ ] Officer consent forms signed by each officer.
- [ ] Contact Person designated and consent obtained (s.113).

## Phase 2: Incorporated Societies Application / 社团注册申请

### 2.1 Online Application (Registrar of Incorporated Societies)
- [ ] Create account on Companies Office / Incorporated Societies register.
- [ ] Complete online application form with:
  - [ ] Society name
  - [ ] Registered office address (NZ physical address required)
  - [ ] Contact Person details (name, email, NZ address)
  - [ ] Upload signed constitution
  - [ ] Upload list of founding members (≥10)
  - [ ] Upload officer consent forms
- [ ] Pay registration fee (check current fee on Companies Office website).
- [ ] Submit application.
- [ ] Certificate of Incorporation received and stored securely.

### 2.2 Post-Incorporation Steps
- [ ] Open a dedicated **bank account** in the Society's name (do not use personal accounts).
- [ ] Obtain IRD number for the Society.
- [ ] Record incorporation number in all official documents and correspondence.

## Phase 3: Charity Registration (Charities Services) / 慈善机构注册申请

### 3.1 Eligibility Check
- [ ] Confirm all purposes are **exclusively charitable** within Charities Act 2005.
- [ ] Charitable purposes fall within recognised categories:
  - [ ] Relief of poverty; OR
  - [ ] Advancement of education; OR
  - [ ] Advancement of religion; OR
  - [ ] **Other purpose beneficial to the community** ✓ *(HCCA's primary basis: cultural heritage and community welfare)*
- [ ] All purposes are for the **public benefit**.
- [ ] Constitution contains the **non-distribution clause** (no financial gain for members).
- [ ] Constitution contains the **asset lock clause** (assets to qualifying NFP on winding up).

### 3.2 Charities Services Application
- [ ] Complete online application at charities.govt.nz.
- [ ] Provide:
  - [ ] Incorporation certificate and number
  - [ ] Constitution
  - [ ] Names and contact details of all officers (Committee members)
  - [ ] Description of activities and how they advance charitable purposes
  - [ ] Bank account details for the charity
- [ ] Provide evidence of charitable activities or a credible implementation plan.
- [ ] List any overseas connections or overseas funding sources.

### 3.3 Post-Registration Obligations (Ongoing)
- [ ] Annual Return submitted to Charities Services by due date each year.
- [ ] Financial statements prepared and filed (tier appropriate to annual expenditure).
- [ ] Notify Charities Services within **20 working days** of any change to:
  - [ ] Officers / Committee members
  - [ ] Contact Person
  - [ ] Registered office / address
  - [ ] Constitutional amendments
  - [ ] Significant change in activities or purposes
