# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

**NZ Chinese Heritage & Community Alliance Inc. (HCCA) | 华裔传承社区联盟**

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

HCCA is committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and transparent community. All officers, members, volunteers, contributors to this repository, and participants at HCCA events are expected to uphold the following standards.

华裔传承社区联盟致力于建立一个包容、尊重与透明的社区环境。所有理事会成员、普通会员、志愿者、本仓库贡献者及活动参与者均须遵守以下行为准则。

---

## Standards / 行为标准

### Expected Behaviour / 期望行为
- Treat all people with dignity and respect regardless of ethnicity, language, gender, age, disability, religion, or socioeconomic background.
- Communicate openly and constructively in both formal governance processes and community interactions.
- Disclose any conflict of interest promptly and in accordance with the HCCA Conflict of Interest Policy.
- Handle all financial matters with integrity and in compliance with the HCCA Financial Control Policy and applicable New Zealand law.
- Respect the confidentiality of member information and sensitive organisational data.

### Unacceptable Behaviour / 不可接受的行为
- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or abuse in any form.
- Misappropriation of HCCA funds or assets for personal financial gain.
- Deliberate concealment of conflicts of interest.
- Breach of member confidentiality.
- Actions that bring the HCCA into disrepute or undermine its charitable purposes.

---

## Reporting / 举报机制

Concerns or complaints should be directed to the Contact Person designated under the Incorporated Societies Act 2022, or to the HCCA Committee via **hcca-compliance@example.org** *(update with actual address before publication)*.

如有疑虑或投诉，请联系依据《2022年社团法》指定的联络人，或通过 **hcca-compliance@example.org** *(正式发布前请替换为实际地址)* 联系理事会。

All reports will be handled in accordance with the [Dispute Resolution Policy](dispute_resolution/dispute_resolution_policy.md).

所有举报将依据[争议解决政策](dispute_resolution/dispute_resolution_policy.md)处理。

---

## Enforcement / 执行

The HCCA Committee reserves the right to take corrective action, including removal from membership or office, consistent with the HCCA Constitution and the Incorporated Societies Act 2022.

理事会保留依据本组织章程及《2022年社团法》采取纠正措施的权利，包括但不限于撤销会员资格或职务。

## Document Language Policy / 文件语言政策

This Code of Conduct is maintained in a bilingual format as a single document, as it applies equally to all members and community participants. All HCCA policy and constitutional documents follow the bilingual structure below:

- **Primary English versions** (`<filename>.md`) are the **legally operative** versions for official submission and governance.
- **Chinese reference versions** (`<filename>_ZH_REF.md`) are provided for reading comprehension and are **reference only**.
- **In case of any discrepancy, the English primary version prevails.**

本行为准则以双语单文件形式维护。HCCA所有政策及章程文件均遵循以下双语结构：
- **英文主版本**（`<filename>.md`）为具有法律效力的**正式版本**，用于官方提交与治理；
- **中文参考版本**（`<filename>_ZH_REF.md`）仅供阅读参考，不具法律效力；
- **若中英文存在任何差异，以英文主版本为准。**
