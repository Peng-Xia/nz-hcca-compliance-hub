# Committee Eligibility & Officer Duties Policy

> **[OFFICIAL — PRIMARY ENGLISH VERSION]** This is the legally operative version for official submission, regulatory reporting, and governance purposes. For the complete Chinese reference translation, see [`committee_eligibility_ZH_REF.md`](./committee_eligibility_ZH_REF.md). **In case of any discrepancy between the English and Chinese versions, this English version prevails.**

**NZ Chinese Heritage & Community Alliance Inc. (HCCA)**

> **Legislative Basis:** Sections 47–61, Incorporated Societies Act 2022

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy sets out the eligibility requirements for HCCA officers, their legal duties under the Act, and the processes for onboarding, monitoring, and removing officers.

---

## 2. Officer Positions

The HCCA Committee consists of the following officer positions:

| Position | Role Summary |
|----------|-------------|
| **President** | Chairs Committee and general meetings; leads the Society's strategic direction; public spokesperson |
| **Vice-President** | Supports and deputises for the President |
| **Secretary** | Maintains registers, records minutes, files statutory returns, manages correspondence |
| **Treasurer** | Manages accounts, prepares financial statements, oversees financial compliance |
| **Ordinary Committee Members (2–4)** | Participate in Committee decisions; may lead subcommittees or working groups |

---

## 3. Eligibility Requirements *(Section 47)*

A person **may not** act as an officer of HCCA if they:

| Disqualification | Statutory Basis |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Are under 18 years of age | s.47(1)(a) |
| Are not a financial member of HCCA | Constitution clause 11.2(b) |
| Are an undischarged bankrupt | s.47(1)(b) |
| Have been prohibited from being an officer under the ISA 2022 or any other enactment | s.47(1)(c) |
| Have been convicted of a crime involving dishonesty within the last 5 years | s.47(1)(d) |
| Are subject to a property order under the Protection of Personal and Property Rights Act 1988 | s.47(1)(e) |
| Have not provided written consent to act as an officer | s.47(2) |

### 3.1 Self-Assessment Declaration

Before taking office, every officer must complete and sign the **Officer Eligibility Declaration Form** (Appendix A below), confirming they meet all eligibility requirements.

### 3.2 Ongoing Eligibility

Officers have a continuing obligation to notify the Committee immediately if they cease to meet any eligibility requirement. In such circumstances, the officer must vacate their office.

## 4. Duties of Officers *(Sections 51–61)*

All HCCA officers must:

### 4.1 Act in Good Faith *(Section 51)*
Act in good faith and in what the officer reasonably believes to be the best interests of the Society.

### 4.2 Exercise Reasonable Care *(Section 52)*
Exercise the care, diligence, and skill that a reasonable person with the officer's knowledge, experience, and powers would exercise in the same circumstances.

### 4.3 Comply with the Act and Constitution *(Section 53)*
Act in accordance with the Incorporated Societies Act 2022, the HCCA Constitution, and all policies of the Society.

### 4.4 Not Misuse Position *(Section 55)*
Not use their position as an officer to gain a financial advantage for themselves or any connected person, or to cause detriment to the Society.

### 4.5 Not Misuse Information *(Section 56)*
Not disclose or use confidential information about the Society's affairs, except as authorised or required by law.

### 4.6 Disclosure of Interests *(Sections 62–73)*
Disclose and manage conflicts of interest in accordance with the [Conflict of Interest Policy](conflict_of_interest_policy.md) and the [Interests Register](interests_register.md).

### 4.7 Financial Duties *(Subpart 2 ISA 2022)*
Ensure the Society does not operate for the financial gain of any member and that all financial affairs comply with the [Financial Control Policy](../finance_&_funding/financial_control_policy.md).

## 5. New Officer Onboarding Checklist

Upon taking office, the Secretary must ensure each new officer:

- [ ] Has signed the **Officer Eligibility Declaration Form** (Appendix A)
- [ ] Has been provided with a copy of this policy
- [ ] Has been provided with copies of all HCCA policies and the Constitution
- [ ] Has reviewed and declared any interests in the [Interests Register](interests_register.md)
- [ ] Has been given access to relevant HCCA accounts and registers (as applicable)
- [ ] Has completed the GitHub onboarding (read access to this repository)
- [ ] Is aware of the [Compliance Git Flow](../GOVERNANCE.md) and their role in it
- [ ] Has been briefed on the [Contact Person](contact_person.md) designation

*A GitHub Issue using the [New Officer Compliance template](../.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/new_officer_compliance.md) must be opened for each new officer.*

---

## 6. Removal of Officers

Officers may be removed:

*(a)* **By resolution** at a general meeting (special resolution — 75% majority), provided the officer has been given 21 days' notice and the opportunity to speak; or
*(b)* **Automatically**, if the officer ceases to meet any eligibility requirement under Section 47.

Removed officers must:
- Return all Society property and documents;
- Be removed from access to all Society accounts, email addresses, and this repository; and
- Update their entries in the Interests Register as appropriate.
