## Section 1 — Pre-Event Planning

### 1.1 Organisational Approval
- [ ] Event concept has been presented to and approved by the Committee.
- [ ] Event aligns with HCCA's charitable purposes (cultural heritage, community support, education, integration).
- [ ] Event budget prepared and approved by the Committee.
- [ ] If overseas funding is to be used for this event: Overseas Funding compliance confirmed with Treasurer.

### 1.2 Venue
- [ ] Venue booked and confirmed in writing.
- [ ] Venue is compliant with the Building Act 2004 (valid Building Warrant of Fitness displayed, if applicable).
- [ ] Venue capacity confirmed and will not be exceeded.
- [ ] Accessibility needs assessed (wheelchair access, hearing loops, etc.).
- [ ] Venue emergency exits identified and accessible.
- [ ] Venue emergency evacuation procedure obtained from venue manager.
- [ ] First aid kit location confirmed with venue.

### 1.3 Legal and Regulatory Requirements
- [ ] Necessary permits or licences obtained (e.g., council event permit if using a public space, food handling permit if serving food).
- [ ] If liquor is to be served: Special Licence obtained from local council (Alcohol Act requirements met).
- [ ] If collecting personal information: Privacy Act 2020 obligations noted; privacy notice prepared for participants.
- [ ] If filming or photographing participants: consent forms prepared.
- [ ] If the event involves children: Child-safe environment principles applied; relevant volunteers hold valid Police vetting (see Volunteer Policy).

### 1.4 Health & Safety *(Health and Safety at Work Act 2015)*
- [ ] HCCA has fulfilled its duties as a PCBU (Person Conducting a Business or Undertaking) where applicable.
- [ ] Hazard identification completed for the venue and activities.
- [ ] Any identified hazards documented with mitigation measures.
- [ ] Emergency contact list prepared (first aider, event coordinator, venue manager, emergency services numbers).
- [ ] Weather contingency plan in place (for outdoor events).

### 1.5 Financial
- [ ] Event budget approved by Committee.
- [ ] Ticket pricing (if applicable) approved by Committee.
- [ ] Revenue collection method approved (cash handling rules, payment platform setup).
- [ ] Expense authorisation process confirmed (who can approve on-the-day costs and up to what limit).
- [ ] Petty cash float (if needed) approved by Treasurer.

---

## Section 2 — During the Event

### 2.1 Arrival
- [ ] Event coordinator arrives at least 60 minutes before start.
- [ ] Emergency exits checked and unobstructed.
- [ ] First aid kit location confirmed.
- [ ] Volunteer briefing completed (roles, emergency procedure, incident reporting).
- [ ] Sign-in register set up (for accountability and emergency roll call).

### 2.2 During the Event
- [ ] Attendance does not exceed venue capacity.
- [ ] Any incidents, injuries, or complaints noted in the Event Incident Log (below).
- [ ] Privacy notice visible / distributed if collecting personal data.
- [ ] Any unplanned costs approved by the designated authorising officer on site.

### 2.3 Revenue and Cash Handling
- [ ] All cash collected counted and verified by two people.
- [ ] Cash receipts documented and deposited into HCCA bank account within **2 business days**.
- [ ] Electronic payment records retained.

---

## Section 3 — Post-Event

### 3.1 Financial
- [ ] Post-event financial summary prepared within 30 days.
- [ ] All receipts and invoices collected and filed.
- [ ] Cash from event deposited (within 2 business days of event).
- [ ] Final budget vs. actual comparison prepared and filed with Treasurer.

### 3.2 Documentation
- [ ] Attendance numbers recorded.
- [ ] This checklist completed and filed with Secretary.
- [ ] Any incident reports filed.
- [ ] Volunteer hours recorded.
- [ ] Participant feedback collected (if applicable).

### 3.3 Review
- [ ] Post-event debrief held with event team.
- [ ] Lessons learned noted for future events.
- [ ] Photos/videos reviewed for privacy compliance before sharing publicly.
