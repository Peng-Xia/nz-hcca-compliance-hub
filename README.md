# NZ Chinese Heritage & Community Alliance Inc.
# 华裔传承社区联盟

**English Abbreviation / 英文简称:** HCCA
**Registered under / 注册依据:** Incorporated Societies Act 2022 (New Zealand)
**Charitable Purpose / 慈善宗旨:** Registered Charity applicant under Charities Act 2005

---

## Organisation Vision / 组织愿景

**English:**
The NZ Chinese Heritage & Community Alliance Inc. (HCCA) exists to strengthen the cultural identity, social inclusion, and community wellbeing of Chinese-heritage communities in New Zealand. We celebrate and preserve Chinese cultural traditions while building bridges between Chinese heritage and New Zealand's multicultural society — ensuring that future generations remain connected to their roots while fully participating in New Zealand civic life.

**中文：**
华裔传承社区联盟（HCCA）致力于增强新西兰华裔社区的文化认同、社会融合与社区福祉。我们传承与弘扬中华文化传统，同时在华裔文化与新西兰多元社会之间架设桥梁——确保下一代在深植文化根脉的同时，全面融入新西兰公民生活。

---

## Mission / 使命

**English:**
- Organise cultural, educational, and social events that celebrate Chinese heritage.
- Provide a support network for newly arrived Chinese immigrants and long-standing community members alike.
- Advocate for the interests of Chinese-heritage communities in New Zealand's policy and civic spaces.
- Operate transparently, accountably, and in strict compliance with New Zealand law.

**中文：**
- 举办传承中华文化的文化、教育与社区活动。
- 为新到达的华裔移民及长期定居社区成员提供支持网络。
- 在新西兰政策与公民社会空间中为华裔社区发声。
- 以透明、问责和严格合规的方式运营。

## Event Organisation Principles / 活动组织原则

**English:**
All HCCA events must adhere to the following principles:

1. **Inclusivity:** Events are open to all community members regardless of background, unless a specific programme rationale requires otherwise and has been approved by the Committee.
2. **Safety:** All events must complete the [Event Safety & Compliance Checklist](event_operations/event_compliance_checklist.md) prior to commencement.
3. **Transparency:** Event budgets and post-event financial summaries must be filed with the Treasurer and made available to members on request.
4. **Volunteer Welfare:** All volunteers are managed in accordance with the [Volunteer Management Policy](event_operations/volunteer_management_policy.md).
5. **Cultural Sensitivity:** Events involving cultural content must be reviewed by a Committee-designated cultural advisor to ensure respectful and accurate representation.

**中文：**
所有HCCA活动须遵循以下原则：

1. **包容性：** 活动向所有社区成员开放，除非特定项目有理由限制参与且已获理事会批准。
2. **安全性：** 所有活动在开始前须完成[活动安全与合规清单](event_operations/event_compliance_checklist.md)。
3. **透明度：** 活动预算及活动后财务摘要须提交财务官备案，并应成员要求予以公开。
4. **志愿者福祉：** 所有志愿者依照[志愿者管理规范](event_operations/volunteer_management_policy.md)进行管理。
5. **文化敏感性：** 涉及文化内容的活动须由理事会指定的文化顾问审查，以确保尊重与准确地呈现文化。

## Overseas Funding Transparency Declaration / 海外赞助透明度声明

**English:**
HCCA may receive funding from overseas donors in support of its charitable purposes. To ensure full transparency and compliance:

- All overseas donations exceeding NZD 1,500 (or equivalent) in a single transaction or NZD 5,000 cumulative from a single overseas source in any 12-month period must be disclosed in the Annual Report and recorded in the Overseas Funding Register.
- HCCA conducts anti-money laundering (AML) self-assessments for all overseas funding receipts consistent with the Anti-Money Laundering and Countering Financing of Terrorism Act 2009 obligations applicable to charities.
- No overseas funds may be distributed to individual members as personal financial gain. All funds must be applied exclusively to the HCCA's charitable purposes.
- Full details of the overseas funding compliance framework are set out in the [Overseas Funding Rules](finance_&_funding/overseas_funding_rules.md).

**中文：**
HCCA 可接受来自海外捐助方的资金，以支持其慈善宗旨。为确保完全透明与合规：

- 单笔超过 NZD 1,500（或等值）或同一海外来源在任何12个月内累计超过 NZD 5,000 的捐款，须在年度报告中披露并记录于海外资金登记册。
- HCCA 对所有海外资金的接收进行反洗钱（AML）自查，符合适用于慈善机构的《2009年反洗钱和反恐怖融资法》相关义务。
- 任何海外资金均不得作为个人经济利益分配给会员，所有资金须专用于 HCCA 的慈善目的。
- 海外资金合规框架的完整细节载于[海外资金合规规则](finance_&_funding/overseas_funding_rules.md)。

## Bilingual Document Policy / 双语文件政策

All policy and constitutional documents in this repository are maintained in **two parallel versions**:

| Version | File Naming | Authority | Purpose |
|---------|------------|-----------|---------|
| **Primary English** | `<filename>.md` | **Legally operative** | Official submission to regulators, governance decisions, legal reference |
| **Chinese Reference** | `<filename>_ZH_REF.md` | **Reference only / 仅供参考** | Member reading comprehension and community communication |

**The English primary version always prevails in case of any discrepancy.**

所有政策及章程文件均维护**两个平行版本**。若中英文版本存在任何差异，**以英文主版本为准**。

**Sync Rule / 同步规则：** Every update to a primary English document must be accompanied by a corresponding update to its `_ZH_REF.md` file in the same Pull Request. See [GOVERNANCE.md Section 6](GOVERNANCE.md) for the full sync policy.

每次更新英文主版本时，必须在同一 Pull Request 中同步更新对应的 `_ZH_REF.md` 文件。
